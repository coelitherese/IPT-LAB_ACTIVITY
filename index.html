PROFESSOR AUREA JUNIPER

Professor Juniper holds a significant place in the Pokémon world as the first female Professor in the Main Series games, a distinction that almost didn't happen, as her character was originally envisioned as male until Ken Sugimori's influential request. Beyond this trailblazing role, Juniper exhibits several unique traits that set her apart. She's the only professor known to permanently keep the Patrat caught during the game's tutorial, which can be found in her lab, and notably, she was responsible for caring for Ash's additional Pokémon in the Unova Region, a task traditionally handled by Professor Oak. Her introduction in Black and White showcases her using a Minccino for battle, a detail that even offers a rare chance for a Shiny encounter in the game's opening.

Juniper's distinctive qualities extend to her very name, as she's the only professor named after a conifer as of Generation 9, diverging from the usual deciduous tree theme. Her physical appearance complements her professional yet approachable demeanor, characterized by her tall stature, fair skin, green eyes, and distinctive poofy honey-colored hair, typically adorned in a pale greenish-blue lab coat. Her consistent presence from the very start of the game, guiding players through the initial choices of First Partner Pokémon and demonstrating essential battle mechanics, solidifies her role as a pivotal and memorable figure in the Pokémon universe.

OSHAWOTT'S ATTACKS

HYDRO PUMP

Razor Shell is Oshawott's signature move and a cornerstone of its combat style. This Water-type physical attack showcases Oshawott's mastery over its scalchop, which it detaches from its belly and wields with surprising dexterity.

The scalchop, imbued with Water energy, becomes incredibly sharp, allowing Oshawott to deliver swift, powerful slashes that can cut through various obstacles and defenses, often leaving opponents stunned by the speed and precision of the attack.

RAZOR SHELL

Hydro Pump is a powerful Water-type special attack that exemplifies Oshawott's potential for raw, concentrated power. Though often learned later in its evolutionary line, a particularly skilled Oshawott might be able to muster the energy to unleash this devastating torrent of water. It involves Oshawott gathering a vast amount of water within its body and expelling it as a high-pressure, focused beam capable of knocking back even the sturdiest of opponents and causing significant damage.

AQUA JET

Aqua Jet is a Water-type priority move that allows Oshawott to close distances rapidly and strike before its opponent can react. By surrounding itself in a concentrated burst of water, Oshawott propels itself forward at incredible speeds, transforming into a living torpedo.

This move is particularly effective for getting out of dangerous situations, dodging incoming attacks, or delivering a decisive finishing blow when speed is of the essence.
